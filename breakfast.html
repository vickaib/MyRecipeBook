Eggs on Toast

Source: https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/50261204731_7bcda0b797_b.jpg

Eggs on Toast is a recipe that anyone can create and customize. With simple ingredients that everyone can find at home, this recipe can be made in a flash. Spruce it up with extra protein, slices of cheese, or fresh herbs from your garden. No matter how you craft this dish, it's guaranteed to be a solid start to your day.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Eggs
  • ...and whatever you'd like!

Note: This recipe is extremely customizable. Feel free to use whatever ingredients that you enjoy the most.

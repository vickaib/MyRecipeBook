What's for Dessert?

We all need a little something sweet.

Raspberry Pistachio Macarons

Source: Raspberry Pistachio Macaron Photo

Raspberry Pistachio Macarons are a delicious, fruity, nutty light dessert that's perfect for the end of the day, or if you're craving something a little sweet. It's a lot of work to make them, but they're definitely worth the effort.

Ingredients:

  • Almond flour
  • Powdered sugar
  • Egg whites
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Pistachios
  • Raspberries

Note: One of my favorite pages that consists mainly of macarons is Pies and Tacos. If you'd like to learn more, definitely check it out!

What's for Lunch?

Here you can find light meals that you can enjoy in the middle of the day.

Charcuterie Board

Source: https://cdn.pixabay.com/photo/2018/12/22/16/36/recipe-3889913_1280.jpg

Charcuterie Boards will always help you feel like an elegant wine mom at a country club. These artistic, edible arrangements are great for groups. Or even with your own company!

Common Ingredients:

  • Smoked Salmon
  • Various Cheeses
  • Prosciutto
  • Sliced bread or crackers
  • Fruits
  • ...and whatever you'd like!

Note: This recipe is a delectable finger food, which would naturally go great with Raspberry Pistachio Macarons.

Roasted Tomato Stew

Source: https://www.maxpixel.net/static/photo/1x/Borsch-A-Simple-Recipe-Cook-At-Home-Food-4260907.jpg

Roasted Tomato Stew brings a comfort to your belly that's unmatched, which is why it's one of my favorite meals. Toss in some protein, some carbs, and a dollop of sour cream and you've got a hearty meal that'll set you up for the day.

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • Protein of choice
  • Red or green peppers
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro

Some recipes to accompany you at the end of your day.

Salmon Steak Dinner

Source: https://stocksnap.io/photo/G8QICMKLUV/editor

Salmon Steak Dinner. Sometimes we need a break from meat. Salmon is a great source of omega-3s and is a great way to end the day.

Ingredients:

  • Salmon Steak
  • Lemon
  • Seasonings of your choice

Classic Pork Ribs and Salad

Source: https://cdn.pixabay.com/photo/2016/06/15/19/09/food-1459693_1280.jpg

Classic Pork Ribs and Salad is a perfect ending to your day. Tender, savory, all paired with refreshing mixed veggies is sure to make you happy.

Ingredients:

  • Pork Ribs
  • Salt and pepper
  • BBQ Sauce (optional)
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Lettuce
